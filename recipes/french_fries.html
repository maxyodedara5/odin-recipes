French Fries

Delicious home made French Fries created in Air fryer

Ingredients

Spices

  • Turmeric (Optional)
  • Black Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Salt

Potatoes

  • Diced potatoes
  • Corn flour

Steps

Fries Preparation

  1. Boil the diced potatoes for 10 mins in salt water with baking soda
  2. Cool the diced potatoes in cold water and dry them using towel
  3. Coat the potatoes with oil, a pinch of corn flour, paprika, black pepper and turmeric

Air Fry

  1. Air fry the potatoes for 25 mins with 180 degree celcius
  2. Shake them midway for evenly air frying them
