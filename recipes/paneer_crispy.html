Paneer Crispy

Delicious restaurant-style Paneer Crispy

Ingredients

Red Chilli Paste
  • Kashmiri Red Chilli - 20-25 (deseeded and soaked)
  • Water
  • Vinegar
Paneer Chunks
  • Paneer
  • Corn flour
Sauce
  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • Chillies
  • Ginger
  • Capsicum
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Soy Sauce
  • Corn flour

Steps

Red Chilli Paste Preparation
  1. Soak the Kashmiri chilli in hot water for 2 hours, grind the chillies into fine paste
  2. Add the paste to an oiled heated pan, stir well, and cook over medium-high flame until crumbly and oil releases
  3. Add vinegar, stir well, store for later usage
Fried Paneer Chunks
  1. Coat the paneer Chunks with corn flour and fry them in oil
  2. Fry them until slightly golden brown
Sauce
  1. Heat the pan, add oil and then over high flame saute Garlic, Ginger, Chillies
  2. Add Capsicum, Onion and saute over medium flame
  3. Add spices pince of salt, pepper, sugar some soy Sauce, vinegar and stir well
  4. Thicken the sauce with corn flour slurry, heat for few minutes
Paneer Crispy
  1. Add fried Paneer Chunks to the sauce
  2. Make sure sauce is coated evenly to all chunks and flash fry for a minute
  3. Add some garnish and serve hot
